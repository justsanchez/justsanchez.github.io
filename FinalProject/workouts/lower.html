To work out the lower body, target the glutes, quadriceps, hamstrings, and calves with compound exercises like squats, lunges, and deadlifts. Don't forget isolation exercises for specific muscles. Maintain proper form, increase weight and intensity gradually, and allow for rest and recovery time.

Lower Body Workouts

Barbell Squat

Muscles worked: Quadriceps, glutes, hamstrings, calves, lower back, and core.

expand screen to show more information...

Description:

  • Begin by standing with your feet shoulder-width apart, with the barbell resting on your upper back.
  • Tighten your core and engage your glutes, then begin to lower your body down by bending your knees and hips, keeping your chest up and your back straight.
  • Continue to lower yourself down until your thighs are parallel to the ground or lower, then pause for a brief moment.
  • Push through your heels and lift your body back up, extending your hips and knees until you return to the starting position.
  • Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

Leg Press (Machine)

Muscles worked: Quads, hamstrings, glutes, calves

expand screen to show more information...

Description:

  • Adjust the seat height so that your knees are at a 90-degree angle when your feet are flat on the footplate.
  • Sit on the seat with your back flat against the backrest and your feet positioned shoulder-width apart on the footplate.
  • Place your hands on the side handles of the machine.
  • Engage your core and push the footplate away from you by extending your legs.
  • Continue pressing until your legs are fully extended, but avoid locking out your knees.
  • Pause briefly, then slowly lower the footplate back to the starting position, bending your knees.
  • Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

Barbell Deadlift

Muscles worked: Hamstrings, glutes, lower back, upper back, grip strength

expand screen to show more information...

Description:

  • Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and position the barbell on the floor in front of you.
  • Bend down and grab the bar with a shoulder-width, overhand grip.
  • Keep your chest up and your back flat, engage your core, and lift the bar off the floor by extending your hips and knees.
  • Keep the bar close to your body throughout the lift and stand up straight, squeezing your glutes at the top.
  • Lower the bar back down to the floor by bending your hips and knees, keeping your back flat.
  • Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

Leg Extension

Muscles worked: Quadriceps (front of thighs)

expand screen to show more information...

Description:

  • Adjust the machine so that the padded bar is just above your ankles.
  • Sit on the machine with your back against the pad and your hands holding onto the side handles.
  • Extend your legs in front of you, straightening your knees to lift the padded bar.
  • Hold at the top of the movement for a brief pause, then lower your legs back down to the starting position.
  • Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

Leg Curl

Muscles worked: Hamstrings (back of thighs)

expand screen to show more information...

Description:

  • Adjust the machine so that the padded bar is just above your ankles.
  • Lie face down on the machine with your knees just off the edge and your legs straight.
  • Place your ankles under the padded bar, holding onto the handles at the front of the machine.
  • Curl your legs up towards your buttocks by bending your knees, squeezing your hamstrings at the top of the movement.
  • Hold for a brief pause, then lower your legs back down to the starting position.
  • Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

Hamstrings, glutes, lower back, and core

Description:

  • Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart, holding a barbell in front of your thighs with an overhand grip.
  • Hinge forward at the hips, pushing your buttocks back and keeping your back straight. The bar should travel down the front of your thighs as you hinge forward.
  • Hold for a brief pause, then lift your torso back up by squeezing your glutes and hamstrings.
  • Keep the bar close to your body as you lift it, maintaining a straight back and hinged hips.
  • Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

Dumbbell Lunges

Muscles worked: Legs, glutes, hamstrings

expand screen to show more information...

Description:

  • Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart, holding a dumbbell in each hand at your sides.
  • Take a big step forward with one foot and lower your body until your front thigh is parallel to the ground, keeping your back straight and your core engaged.
  • Push back up through your front heel and return to the starting position, then repeat on the other side.
  • Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.
