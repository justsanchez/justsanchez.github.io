To work out the core, engage the abdominals, lower back, and hips with compound exercises like planks, sit-ups, and leg raises. Don't forget isolation exercises for specific muscles. Maintain proper form, increase difficulty gradually, and vary the exercises for a challenging workout.

Core Body Workouts (Without Equipment)

Dragon Flags (Leg Raises)

Muscles worked: Rectus abdominis, obliques, hip flexors, lower back muscles

expand screen to show more information...

Description:

  • Lie on your back on a bench (or yoga pad) or a stable surface, with your head towards the bench and your hands grasping the bench behind your head for support.
  • Lift your legs up and bend your knees to a 90-degree angle, with your thighs perpendicular to your torso.
  • Engage your core and lift your hips and lower back off the bench, straightening your legs and lifting them towards the ceiling.
  • Keep your legs straight and your toes pointed, and lower them back down towards the bench with control.
  • Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

Crunches

Muscles worked: Rectus abdominis

expand screen to show more information...

Description:

  • Lie on your back on a mat or a comfortable surface, with your knees bent and your feet flat on the ground.
  • Place your hands behind your head with your elbows out to the sides.
  • Engage your core muscles and lift your shoulder blades off the ground, curling your upper body towards your knees.
  • Pause briefly at the top of the movement, then lower your upper body back down to the ground.
  • Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

Cross Crunch

Muscles worked: Rectus abdominis, obliques

expand screen to show more information...

Description:

  • Lie on your back on a mat or a comfortable surface, with your knees bent and your feet flat on the ground.
  • Place your hands behind your head with your elbows out to the sides.
  • Lift your shoulder blades off the ground and engage your core muscles.
  • Twist your torso to the left and bring your right elbow towards your left knee, while extending your right leg out straight.
  • Lower your shoulder blades back down to the ground and repeat on the other side, bringing your left elbow towards your right knee while extending your left leg out straight.
  • Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

Bicycle Crunches

Muscles worked: Rectus abdominis, obliques

expand screen to show more information...

Description:

  • Lie on your back on a mat or a comfortable surface, with your knees bent and your feet flat on the ground.
  • Place your hands behind your head with your elbows out to the sides.
  • Engage your core muscles and lift your shoulder blades off the ground, curling your upper body towards your knees.
  • Twist your torso to the left and bring your right elbow towards your left knee, while extending your right leg out straight.
  • Switch sides and twist your torso to the right, bringing your left elbow towards your right knee while extending your left leg out straight.
  • Alternate sides in a cycling motion, keeping your core engaged throughout the movement.
  • Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

Core Body Workouts (With Equipment)

Kneeling Cable Crunches

Muscles worked: Rectus abdominis, obliques

expand screen to show more information...

Description:

  • Attach a rope handle to a high pulley on a cable machine, and kneel facing away from the machine with the rope handle behind your head.
  • Grasp the rope handle with both hands and place your hands on either side of your head.
  • Engage your core muscles and slowly crunch forward, bringing your elbows towards your knees.
  • Pause briefly at the bottom of the movement, then slowly return to the starting position.
  • Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

Standing Cable Crunches

Muscles worked: Rectus abdominis, obliques

expand screen to show more information...

Description:

  • Attach a rope handle to a high pulley on a cable machine, and stand facing away from the machine with the rope handle behind your head.
  • Grasp the rope handle with both hands and place your hands on either side of your head.
  • Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart, and engage your core muscles.
  • Slowly crunch forward, bringing your elbows towards your knees.
  • Pause briefly at the bottom of the movement, then slowly return to the starting position.
  • Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

Decline Sit-Ups

Muscles worked: Rectus abdominis, obliques, hip flexors

expand screen to show more information...

Description:

  • Position yourself on a decline bench with your feet securely locked in place.
  • Lie on your back and cross your arms over your chest or place them behind your head.
  • Engage your core muscles and lift your upper body towards your knees.
  • Pause briefly at the top of the movement, then slowly lower yourself back down to the starting position.
  • Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

Incline Leg Hip Raises

Muscles worked: Lower abs, hip flexors

expand screen to show more information...

Description:

  • Position yourself on an incline bench with your head at the top and your feet securely locked in place at the bottom.
  • Lie on your back and extend your legs towards the ceiling, keeping them straight and together.
  • Place your hands on the bench at your sides for support.
  • Engage your lower abs and hip flexors, and lift your hips off the bench towards the ceiling.
  • Pause briefly at the top of the movement, then slowly lower your hips back down to the starting position.
  • Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.
