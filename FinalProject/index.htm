Whats is Muscle Hypertrophy?

Muscle Hypertrophy* is the process of increasing muscle size through resistance training or other types of physical activity that challenge the muscles. It involves structural changes in muscle fibers, resulting in an increase in overall muscle size, strength, and endurance. The type and intensity of training required to induce hypertrophy may vary depending on individual factors.
